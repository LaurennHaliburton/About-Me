Favorite Foods

Lauren Haliburton is a foodie with a love for bold flavors and comforting classics. She can never say no to a perfectly cheesy pizza or a juicy burger, and she has a soft spot for the rich, savory goodness of crawfish étouffée. Her sweet tooth craves the chewy delight of mochi, while papa rellena brings her the ultimate crispy-yet-creamy satisfaction. Whether indulging in a favorite dish or trying something new, Lauren believes that good food is one of life’s greatest joys.

  2. Mochi
  3. Papa Relleno
  4. Crawfish Etouffee
  5. Burgers
